CherryBum

• Project on Figma

Vermicomposting website

This project was meant to practice the knowledge acquired in the responsive web design certification for FreeCodeCamp; I decided to give it a purpose since me and my partner were thinking of selling worms for compost. It has the option for the user to submit their email to receive more detailed information and to make the transaction easier. It displays a video on how worms transmute food into nutrients and buttons to pick the amount of worms they want. Finally, it has a phone number where they can request worms via sms.
